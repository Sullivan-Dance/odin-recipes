Burger

Description

This recipe will guide you on how you can make a juicy burger from scratch.

This was designed with beginners in mind, ensuring anyone can enjoy this filling treat.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • American Cheese
  • Burger Buns
  • Ground Beef
  • Butter
  • Ketchup
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Roll a small piece of the ground beef into a ball.
  2. Place the beef in a skillet and press on it until cooked.
  3. Spread butter on the buns and toast them.
  4. Cut tomatoes into slices and get pieces of lettuce.
  5. Spread ketchup on the buns and stack the beef, tomatoes and lettuce.
  6. Add melted cheese on top and close the burger.
  7. Serve hot.
