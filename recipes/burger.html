Burger

Description

This recipe will guide you on how you can make a juicy burger from scratch.

This was designed with beginners in mind, ensuring anyone can enjoy this filling treat.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • American Cheese
  • Burger Buns
  • Ground Beef
  • Butter
  • Ketchup
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. You mix the flour with water and yeast, making sure the yeast is alive.
  2. Do this in a bowl and cover with plastic wrap and leave to rise
  3. In the meantime, crush the tomatoes into a fine paste and boil them. Add salt and pepper as well.
  4. Take the dough and round it into a pie.
  5. Add a drop of sauce and spread evenly, then add the cheese.
  6. Bake in the oven at 360 and brush with olive oil.
  7. Serve hot.
