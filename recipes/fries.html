French Fries

Description

This recipe will guide you on how you can make some tasty seasoned french fries from scratch.

This was designed with beginners in mind, ensuring anyone can enjoy this delectable delight.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cajun Spice
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into cuboids.
  2. Soak the cut pieces in a bowl of water for a few hours.
  3. Drain the water and pat the potatoes dry.
  4. Fry the potatoes in oil at a temperature of 360°.
  5. Mix salt, pepper and cajun spice in a bowl.
  6. Mix the spice mix with the French Fries.
  7. Serve hot.
